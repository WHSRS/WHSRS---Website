Our Services

Comprehensive Women's Health Exams

Our expert team offers a full range of gynecological services, including comprehensive women's health exams, pap smears, and clinical breast exams. We believe in empowering women to take control of their health and offer patient-centered care with a focus on education and prevention.

Birth Control

At Women's Health Services of Roaring Spring, we understand that family planning is an important part of life. We offer counseling and prescribing of many forms of birth control including injections, IUDs, implants, oral contraceptives, and patches.

Minimally Invasive Office Procedures

Our team of experienced healthcare professionals specialize in minimally invasive in-office procedures, including colposcopies and biopsies. These procedures offer many benefits including less pain, easier recovery, and a quicker diagnosis.

Genitourinary Concerns

We offer non-surgical treatment for pelvic organ prolapse including pessary fitting and management, along with referrals to Urology for surgical needs. We also offer pelvic floor therapy referrals for those who need help targeting those core muscles.

Menopause Management

Our team provides comprehensive menopause management services to help women navigate through this transitional phase of life. We offer individualized treatment plans to manage symptoms such as hot flashes, mood changes, and vaginal dryness.

Sexual Health and Wellness

At Women's Health Services of Roaring Spring, we believe that sexual health and wellness are an important part of a women's overall wellbeing. We offer services such as STD testing and counseling to help our patients maintain optimal gynecological health.
