About Us

At our clinic, we are dedicated to excellence in women’s healthcare. We offer a comprehensive range of gynecological and laboratory services in a respectful, supportive, and state-of-the-art environment. Our mission is to provide personalized, evidence-based care that empowers women at every stage of life. Through compassion, education, and trust, we strive to support each patient’s health journey with the highest standards of medical expertise and individualized attention.

Dr. Stephen J. Bisacco, M.D., F.A.C.O.G

Dr Bisacco presents as a well-seasoned, trained, and experienced Gynecologist at Women's Health Services of Roaring Spring. His role is collaborating with the CRNP in diagnosing and treating the common problems affecting women and providing the expertise of any necessary office procedures. In his previous practice, he pioneered many advancements in women's care, such as the use of in-office laser treatments, the early use of laparoscopic surgery, the study, purpose, and use of in-office sonohysterography (published), and the introduction and success of the Allied Health Professional. He is dedicated to the treatment of women and their success in the medical profession.
